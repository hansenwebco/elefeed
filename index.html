Elefeed · Privacy Policy
Tidy Feeds

Feeds your way

Switch effortlessly between posts from people you follow or curated feeds of your favorite hashtags. Elefeed keeps your timeline organized and focused on what matters most to you.

Visual Context

Know exactly why

Every post in your feed is color-coded and clearly notated. Whether it's a hashtag you follow or a profile recommendation, you'll never wonder why a post appeared in your timeline.

Always with you

Mobile-First Experience

Elefeed is built for the modern web. Install it as a Progressive Web App (PWA) to get a native-app feel on your iPhone or Android. Fast, responsive, and ready for your home screen.

Privacy First

Private, local, and fast

Elefeed is a lightweight client that runs entirely in your browser. No databases, no tracking, and no third-party servers. All your data stays in your browser's local storage.

Custom Experience

Tailored to You

Customize every aspect of your experience. Control media previews, filter by language, manage instant notifications, and switch between light and dark modes. Elefeed is built to adapt perfectly to your flow.

Elefeed is open source. Contribute or view the code on GitHub.
